# Domain and Literature Review Report

Decision: Major Revision.

The paper correctly distinguishes training-only privileged CT supervision from inference-time multimodal fusion. It must better position itself against recent privileged-modality and cross-modal medical-transfer work, including DANTE, K-MaT, and MICCAI 2025 CXR-CTPA cross-modal KD. It must also disclose and differentiate the authors' March 2026 arXiv pilot.

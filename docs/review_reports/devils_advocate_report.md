# Devil's Advocate Review Report

Decision: Major Revision.

The strongest counterargument is that the two positive cells may be same-cohort tuning artifacts rather than robust privileged-modality transfer. The method searches teacher views, confidence thresholds, auxiliary weights, and target types on one public paired cohort with 113 positives. Without external paired validation and stronger patient-level uncertainty, the result should be framed as same-cohort mechanism evidence, not broad validation.

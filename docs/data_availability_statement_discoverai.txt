Data Availability Statement (Discover Artificial Intelligence submission)

Selection: Yes. I used or generated research data in this study.

Statement text:
The BIMCV-COVID19+ imaging data used in this study are publicly available
from the original data custodians (Valencian Region Medical ImageBank) at
http://bimcv.cipf.es/bimcv-projects/bimcv-covid19 under their release terms;
no pixel data are redistributed by the authors. The patient-level split
definitions, aggregate result tables, and analysis manifests that support
the findings of this study are available at
https://github.com/mabo1215/JDCNET.git.
